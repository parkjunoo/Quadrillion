## Source Notes

- 1970-1990 are pre-1994 public estimates and should be treated as broad comparable estimates, not audited totals.
- 1978 uses the more concrete official estimate; some secondary sources cite a much higher estimate.
- 2026 uses the midpoint of the requested $6.4B-$6.5B estimate and is already treated as 2026 USD.
